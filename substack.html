The Brownian Notion

The Brownian Notion is a newsletter dedicated to bioinformatics, evolutionary biology, genomics, data analysis, and academic research, with a focus on how quantitative and comparative thinking can help us understand biological complexity.

The newsletter explores topics ranging from evolutionary models and phylogenetic methods to comparative genomics, genome–phenome relationships, and the development of computational tools for biological research. Alongside methodological discussions, The Brownian Notion also covers practical aspects of academic life, including research design, data interpretation, publishing, and the everyday realities of working in science.

The Brownian Notion is free and fully open: all content is accessible without paywalls, in the spirit of open science and knowledge sharing. If you find the project useful and would like to support its continuation, you can do so voluntarily via Ko-fi.
